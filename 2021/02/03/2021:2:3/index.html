Exercise of Passage Writing

2021-02-03 passages passages Comments

I am Wu Kefei from Nanjing, China. My mother school is Jinling High School, which is also a Cambridge A-Level centre.

When other people talk about me, the very first word they may talk about is reliable. My family members say that I am a little bit careless but quite reliable in major events. Actually, I sometimes make strange mistakes, for instance taking the subway in the wrong direction. But my classmates think I am more meticulous and can observe some details that are difficult to notice. In their opinions, I am an optimistic girl who likes cats, is gentle to others, and is sometimes silly. In general, my classmates have a relatively good impression of me. My community doesn’t think I’m very enthusiastic. But anyone who needs help can come to me and I will try my best to solve the problem. Of course, before I help others, I will do my job well.

One thing I am very impressed and proud of is that I once won a medal for my school in the Dragon Boat Race in high school. At that time, I was chosen to train for the dragon boat team. But unfortunately, because I had had an injury two months before training, I had not taken part in physical exercise during that period, so it was difficult to play to the normal level. Later, I took part in training with one of my classmates (she is an athlete, so she has a lot of experience in improving her physical fitness). After two months of striving, we got good results in the Dragon Boat Race, ranking second. This experience not only told me the importance of persistence, but also told me to learn to improve with my friends. Maybe the race can change my life afterwards.
